We are formed of a small team. We started in crypto way back in late 2017. We designed this project based on our real experience in the market. Many of us observed and experienced getting REKT (Wrecked) in crypto in many different ways.

  1. We were unable to receive the token we invested in pre-sale.
  2. The Developer did send us the token but didn't put it on the market.
  3. The token is on the market but the developer is manipulating the price since they are holding a huge amount of token by dumping it which is allocated in different purposes, such as Marketing or Development.
However, We couldn't say that the allocated token is only the Dev's token. There were some wallets that the Developer owns, which they could use to dump on the Investors/Traders. This is something that we want to prevent to ensure that the investors of our project are worry-free by creating a low risk platform through transparency and commitment.
